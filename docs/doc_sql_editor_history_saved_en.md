# History & Saved Queries

Never lose your work with the integrated history and saved query management system.

## Query History

Every executed query is automatically logged in the **History** tab of the left sidebar.

- **Search**: Filter history by query keywords.
- **Restore**: Click any history item to instantly load it back into the editor.
- **Retention**: History is stored locally, ensuring your recent work is preserved across sessions.

## Saved Queries

Save your most frequent or complex queries for quick access.

- **Creating**: Click the "Save" icon in the toolbar. You can provide a name and description.
- **Sharing**: Mark a saved query as **Public** to make it accessible to other users in your organization (Admins only).
- **Management**: Rename or delete saved queries directly from the sidebar.

## Tabs

Work on multiple tasks simultaneously using Tabs. double-click a tab to rename it for better organization (e.g., "User Analysis", "Revenue Check").
